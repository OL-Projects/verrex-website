# VERREX - Product Context

## Why This Project Exists
VERREX is a premium **window and door corporation** website for a new high-incorporation business. The website serves as the primary digital presence, handling client relations, product catalog browsing, quote/estimation requests, appointment scheduling, installations coordination, and partner/affiliate management.

## Business Identity
- **Name**: VERREX
- **Industry**: Windows & Doors (Fenestration)
- **Type**: High incorporation / B2B-focused with B2C capability
- **Location**: Toronto, Ontario, Canada
- **Branding**: "Premium Windows & Doors" — "Engineered for Every Project"
- **GitHub**: `OL-Projects/verrex-website`
- **Deployed**: Vercel (auto-deploy from GitHub main branch)

## What Problems It Solves
- **Client Acquisition**: Professional B2B web presence targeting developers, architects, facility managers, and homeowners
- **Product Discovery**: Searchable product catalog + 98-page manufacturer catalog viewer
- **Quote Management**: Multi-step quote wizard + quick hero quote form for fast lead capture
- **Appointment Booking**: Online scheduling for consultations, measurements, inspections, and installations
- **Service Showcase**: Full service lifecycle from consultation to installation
- **Partner Relations**: Dedicated section for manufacturers and affiliate partners
- **Trust & Credibility**: Certification badges (AAMA, NFRC, Energy Star, WDMA), project portfolio, testimonials

## How It Should Work
- **Homepage**: Split hero (content left + glassmorphism quote form right), stats bar, image-based category cards, featured products, services, testimonials, projects showcase, CTA
- **Products Page**: Filterable/searchable catalog with category tabs, pricing, detail pages
- **Catalog Page**: 98-page manufacturer PDF catalog viewer with page-by-page image display
- **Services Page**: 6 service cards with 5-step process timeline
- **Quote Page**: 4-step wizard (Contact Info → Project Details → Additional Info → Review)
- **Appointments Page**: 5 appointment types, date/time picker, contact info
- **Projects Page**: Portfolio showcase with 8 completed projects, testimonials, category filters
- **Contact Page**: Contact form, business hours, map, multiple contact methods
- **About Page**: Mission, values, team bios, partners section
- **Search Page**: Global site search across all pages and content
